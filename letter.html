Letter

Dear Future Wife,
   I want you to know, that I love you. I will cherish you and I will
do my best to take care of you for the rest of my life. I will be handful
at times, and I may not know what I've done wrong, but I won't go anywhere.
I'm not going to leave, and I'll do my best to become a better person,
someone worthy of you. I'll fix my mistakes and do my best to keep you happy.
I'm a little stubborn, but I'll try to keep an open mind and try new things.
I want to grow with you and start a family with you. You are the most important
person to me, and I want to spend the rest of my life with you. I don't
have a lot to offer, but I will give you everything I can. I love you.
I always have, and I always will.
